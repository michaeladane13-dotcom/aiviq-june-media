About Daniel

Artist and psychic reader

Daniel is a Bulgarian Roma psychic artist based in Varna.

He has drawn for most of his life. His psychic readings and drawings developed together, with faces, marks and symbols often appearing while he works.

Every reading and commissioned drawing is handled personally by Daniel.
