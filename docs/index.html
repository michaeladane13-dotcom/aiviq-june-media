About Daniel

Art has always been how spirit speaks through me

From a young age I saw faces in my mind's eye — faces of people my family and friends hadn't yet met, but soon would. As I trained my gift through years of meditation and study, I learned to channel those visions onto paper.

Every reading begins in stillness. I focus on your name and your energy, ask my guides to show me who stands on your path, and sketch exactly what I'm shown — the eyes, the bone structure, the feeling of them. Alongside your portrait, I write down every impression I receive: initials, timing, how you'll meet, and what your connection is here to teach you.

Thousands of drawings later, the moment that never gets old is the message that says: "I found them. It's the face you drew."

Questions

Frequently Asked

What do you need from me for a reading?

Just your first name and date of birth. Some clients like to add a short note about their situation — welcome, but never required. I intentionally read with as little information as possible so what comes through is pure.

How is my drawing delivered?

Digitally, as a high-resolution image along with your full written reading, sent through Etsy messages within 24–48 hours of your order (or within 12 hours with same-day delivery).

Is this a real hand-drawn portrait?

Yes. Every portrait is sketched by hand during your reading. No two drawings are alike, because no two paths are alike.

What if I'm already in a relationship?

Many clients are. Often the face I'm shown is the person already beside you — a beautiful confirmation. When it isn't, the reading speaks to what your soul's path is asking of you. I always deliver what I see with honesty and care.

Can I ask follow-up questions?

Of course. Message me on Etsy after your reading and I'll happily clarify anything that came through.
